FAQ


    How do I register myself?
    You can register yourself with the link provided at the top of this web page.

    What is the eligibility criteria?
    Any student(s) in grade 9 to grade 12 regardless of their age can participate; provided they are a part of a school.

    Till when can I register myself?
    The last date of registration is 20th November 2023.

    What all perks do I get by participating?
    Perks include certificate of participation and/or merit, exciting prizes worth rupees 50,000 and fruitful learning experiences.

    Are the models supposed to be construed on the venue or be preconstructed?
    Models are supposed to be preconstructed which can be assembled at the venue.

    Can I participate in more than one event?
    Yes, you can participate in more than one event.

    Can I participate in model making on an individual basis?
    Yes, you can participate in model making on an individual basis i.e, without a team.

    If my payment fails, what do I do?
    You can email us regarding the issue you are facing but please keep in mind that the University is not responsible for the payment failure.

    If I have queries, whom do I contact?
    One can contact us for any query or information at E-mail address anveshan@svvv.edu.in or can get in touch with any of our student coordinators.
